Tears of Themis

Background setting

Player takes the role of an unnamed 24 years old female lawyer at Themis Legal Office, as she defends her clients in a criminal case. An overarching mystery plays out over the course of every main story chapter. Four different bachelors assist her with the cases, which is where the romantic elements of the story are introduced.
